Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

ContentManager_Auto ("the Service," "we," "us," or "our") is a professional content orchestration platform that enables marketing teams, digital agencies, brands, and content creators to automate video publishing workflows on TikTok via the official TikTok Content Posting API.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect from our business customers and authorized end-users, how we process it, the legal bases for processing, how long we retain it, with whom we share it, and the rights you have over your data. It applies to all users of the Service worldwide.

By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you must not use the Service.

2. Who We Are (Data Controller)

ContentManager_Auto operates the Service. For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data protection rights, contact us at dungeonmaster337@gmail.com.

3. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Users who authenticate via TikTok Login Kit to connect their TikTok accounts to the Service;
  • Business customers (marketing teams, agencies, brands) using the Service to publish content;
  • Visitors to our website.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to TikTok itself. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy, available at tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the Service.

4.1 Data obtained via TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API

  • TikTok open_id (anonymous identifier issued by TikTok)
  • TikTok union_id (where applicable)
  • Display name and username of the authorized TikTok account
  • Profile avatar URL
  • OAuth 2.0 access tokens and refresh tokens issued by TikTok
  • Granted scopes (e.g., video.publish, video.upload, user.info.basic)
  • Publishing status, post identifiers, and error responses returned by the TikTok API

4.2 Content data

  • Video files, captions, hashtags, cover thumbnails, and metadata provided by the customer for publication
  • Scheduling information (planned publish time, target account)

4.3 Account and billing data

  • Email address, organization name, and contact details of the customer
  • Authentication credentials for the Service (hashed)
  • Billing records, invoices, and payment metadata (payments are processed by third-party payment providers; we do not store full card numbers)

4.4 Technical and usage data

  • IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system
  • Log files, access timestamps, API request and response metadata
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 11)

4.5 Categories we do not collect

  • We do not collect TikTok passwords (authentication is delegated to TikTok via OAuth 2.0).
  • We do not collect special categories of personal data (race, religion, health, biometric data, etc.).
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children (see Section 13).

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We disclose data only in the following limited cases:

  • TikTok, as necessary to perform publishing operations you authorize (this is the core function of the Service).
  • Sub-processors strictly necessary to operate the Service: cloud hosting providers, transactional email providers, payment processors. All sub-processors are bound by written agreements requiring confidentiality and at least equivalent data protection. A current list is available on request.
  • Legal disclosures when required by valid legal process, court order, or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers, in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with prior notice to affected users.

7. International Data Transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we rely on appropriate safeguards under GDPR Art. 46, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission and supplementary measures where necessary.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as needed for the purposes described above:

  • TikTok access tokens: until you revoke access or the token expires per TikTok policy.
  • Video files submitted for publishing: deleted within 30 days after successful publication or failed final retry.
  • Publishing logs and post metadata: up to 24 months for service operation, audit, and dispute resolution.
  • Account and billing data: for the lifetime of the customer account plus the period required by applicable tax and accounting law (typically up to 7 years).
  • Technical logs: up to 12 months.

After the retention period ends, data is deleted or irreversibly anonymized.

9. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) for all communications with TikTok and end-users.
  • Encryption at rest for access tokens and credentials.
  • Access control on a need-to-know basis.
  • Logging and monitoring of access to production systems.
  • Regular review of security practices.

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a personal data breach affecting your rights, we will notify the competent supervisory authority within 72 hours where required and inform affected users without undue delay.

10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erase your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA) additionally have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, the right to correct, the right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do not sell or share for cross-context behavioral advertising), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

Revoking TikTok access: you may revoke ContentManager_Auto's access to your TikTok account at any time at tiktok.com/setting under "Manage app permissions." Upon revocation, the associated access tokens are invalidated immediately and we will cease all further processing of data obtained via that authorization.

To exercise any right, contact dungeonmaster337@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
