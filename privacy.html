Privacy Policy

ContentManager_Auto

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Introduction

ContentManager_Auto ("the Application", "we", "us") is a professional content orchestration platform that enables marketing teams and digital agencies to automate video publishing to TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how ContentManager_Auto collects, uses, and protects your data.

Data We Collect

ContentManager_Auto collects the following data to provide its services: TikTok account ID and username (via TikTok Login Kit), video content and metadata provided by the user, and publishing status information returned by TikTok API.

How We Use Your Data

ContentManager_Auto uses collected data solely to authenticate your TikTok account and publish video content on your behalf via the TikTok Content Posting API. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Data Storage

ContentManager_Auto does not permanently store personal data or video content on our servers. Video files are processed in transit and delivered directly to TikTok. Access tokens are stored securely and used only for authorized publishing operations.

Your Rights

You may revoke ContentManager_Auto's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's security settings. Upon revocation, all associated access tokens are invalidated immediately.
